## Fit and measurement: the biggest reason people go custom
A roller shade that’s even slightly off can look crooked, leak light, or scrape the window casing.

### Why fit matters more than you think
Small sizing differences can create big usability issues:

- **Light gaps** on the sides, especially for bedrooms and media rooms
- **Uneven roll** if the shade isn’t level or the width isn’t right
- **Interference** with handles, locks, and trim on doors and casements

### Inside mount vs outside mount: where custom simplifies the decision
Home Depot’s in-stock selection tends to push you toward what’s easiest to sell broadly. Custom lets you choose what’s best for your window and your goals:

- **Inside mount**: cleaner look, more “built-in,” depends on depth
- **Outside mount**: better light control, hides imperfect frames, works with shallow depth
