## The realistic timeline for custom roller shades

Most homeowners should plan on roughly **2 to 6 weeks** from finalizing selections to having custom roller shades ready to install, depending on product choices, availability, and whether you’re doing professional installation or DIY.

Here’s the bigger picture:

- **Research + measuring:** Same day to a few days
- **Swatches and decision-making:** 2 to 7 days (sometimes longer if you want to test in different lighting)
- **Order review and confirmation:** 1 to 2 business days
- **Production:** Often 7 to 15 business days (varies by fabric and options)
- **Shipping:** Commonly 2 to 7 days, depending on carrier and distance
- **Installation:** Same day (DIY) or scheduled within a week (pro install)

## Step 1: Measuring your windows (and why this can be fast)

Measuring is usually the quickest part, but it’s also where a lot of delays start.

If you already know whether you want an inside mount or outside mount, you can measure most windows in 15–30 minutes. If you’re unsure, set aside a little extra time to evaluate depth, obstructions, and how much light gap you can tolerate.

A few tips that keep your timeline on track:

- Measure each window at multiple points, not just once.
- Label every measurement clearly (room + window location).
- Decide early whether you want maximum light blocking or a cleaner minimalist look.

## Step 3: Finalizing your order (1–2 business days)

Once you choose fabric, control type, and any upgrades, the order stage is typically quick.

At this point, you’ll confirm details like:

- Shade type (light filtering, solar, blackout)
- Openness or light control goals
- Roll direction (standard vs reverse)
- Hardware color and valance options

## Step 4: Production time (the biggest variable)

Production is where true custom shades differ from off-the-shelf products.

A typical custom production window is **about 1 to 3 weeks**, but it can shift based on:

- Fabric availability and dye lots
- Motorization and smart-home components
- Hardware finishes and special brackets
- Size and complexity (very wide shades, specialty windows)

## Step 5: Shipping and delivery (2–7 days for most orders)

After production, shipping usually takes a few days to a week.

To avoid last-minute stress:

- Order a little earlier if you’re near a holiday or peak renovation season.
- Consider where the package will be delivered (especially if you’re moving).
- Plan a buffer if your installer needs to schedule in advance.

If you’re working with large windows or extra-wide shades, packaging and freight handling can also impact the delivery window. This guide is helpful if you’re in that situation: [Roller Shades for Large Windows](/blog/roller-shades-for-large-windows).

DIY installation is often same-day once the shades arrive, especially for standard roller shades.

If you’re scheduling installation help, the timeline can be influenced by:

- Installer availability in your area
- How many windows you’re doing
- Whether you need ladder work or high ceilings

## How to shorten your custom shade timeline

If you need your shades sooner, focus on the steps you actually control.

### Decide your priorities before you shop

Pick the top two goals:

- Privacy
- Glare control
- Heat reduction
- Total darkness
- A specific design style

This prevents you from restarting the selection process halfway through.

### Order swatches immediately

Swatches are the fastest way to eliminate uncertainty. Even if you’re 80% sure, ordering swatches right away keeps the project moving.

### Use a checklist for each room

For every window, note:

- Room name
- Mount type preference
- Width and height
- Any obstacles (handles, trim, cranks)
- Light needs (blackout vs light filtering)

That information makes your order faster and reduces back-and-forth.

If you have unusual windows, very large sizes, or you’re unsure about mount depth, it’s quicker to ask first than to pause later.

## FAQ: custom roller shades lead time

### How long do custom roller shades take from order to delivery?

Many orders land in the **2–6 week** range from final order to delivery, depending on fabric, options, and shipping distance.

### Do motorized shades take longer than manual shades?

They can. Motorization adds components and configuration steps, so it’s wise to build in extra time if you’re choosing smart-home or rechargeable motors.

If you’re considering motorization, this guide can help you pick features up front: [Smart Home Motorized Shades Setup](/blog/smart-home-motorized-shades-setup).

### What slows down custom shade orders the most?

The most common delays come from decision changes (color/opacity), measurement uncertainty, and scheduling installation help.

### When should I order shades for a move-in date?

If you have a fixed move-in date, ordering **6–8 weeks ahead** is a comfortable buffer, especially if you want swatches and need installation scheduled.

### Can I order swatches while I’m still measuring?

Yes, and it’s often smart. You can compare swatches in the room while you finalize mount type and measurement details.
