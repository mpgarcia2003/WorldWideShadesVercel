## How each option affects the “visual height” of your windows
This is a subtle but important design detail.

- **Roller shades** tend to make windows feel taller because there’s minimal bulk when raised.
- **Roman shades** add a fabric stack at the top, which can visually shorten the glass if the window is small.

## Style comparison: clean lines vs. tailored folds

## Roller shades: modern, minimal, consistent
Roller shades disappear when raised and create a crisp plane when lowered. They’re a strong match for contemporary interiors, open-plan homes, and spaces where you want the window to feel calm.

If you’re building a cohesive look across a home, roller shades are easier to standardize across rooms.

## Roman shades: warm, decorative, and texture-forward
Roman shades bring softness and depth. They can add character to a room that feels too “boxy,” especially with textured fabric.
